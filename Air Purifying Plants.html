Air Purifying Plants

INTRODUCTION

Air-purifying plants are indoor plants that naturally cleanse the air by absorbing toxins, chemicals, and pollutants through their leaves and roots. They help reduce indoor air pollution, which can come from everyday household items like furniture, cleaning products, and even electronics. In addition to absorbing harmful substances, these plants release oxygen, improving overall air quality and contributing to a healthier indoor environment. They filter out harmful chemicals like formaldehyde, benzene, and trichloroethylene.By photosynthesis, they increase the oxygen level in the air, which can enhance mood and productivity.Many air-purifying plants also release moisture into the air, helping to maintain a comfortable humidity level.The presence of greenery has been shown to reduce stress and improve mental well-being. Air-purifying plants are an effective, natural way to enhance indoor air quality while adding beauty and tranquility to your living space.
